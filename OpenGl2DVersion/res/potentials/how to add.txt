To add new potentials please use the "empty_with_border.bmp" template. 
Requierements for the potential images:
-filename.bmp
-512*512 Resolution
-32bit A8R8G8B8
-BitmapV5Header
-compression type: Bitfields
-the blue channel is used for the potential, all other channels are ignored
-only supports up to 20 images in the folder potentials
GIMP2 has been used to generate these images
